NOTE: Infered from OpenWeather https://openweathermap.org/forecast5
Free subscription gives access for 3 hour 5 day forecast

Required
- weather (all of it e.g. weather.id)
- main (all bar maybe sea level and ground level)
- wind (all)
- pop (prob of precipitation)
- dt_txt (date as txt - YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS)
- sunrise, sunset

Nice to have
- clouds.all (Cloud Cover) (%)
- visibility (Meters)
- rain (amount in 3 hours - may generalise)
- country

Not Wanted
- temp_fk
- population
